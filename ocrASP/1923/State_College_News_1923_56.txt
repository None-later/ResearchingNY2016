COLLEGE CONCERT

On Friday evening, May 11, the
college choruses will give a concert,
probably in the college auditorium.
The orchestra will, do its share as Will
the men's chorus. he women’s
chorus will present folk songs of the
British Isles, consisting of English.
Scotch, and Irish folk songs, one of
which will be the Skye Boat song
which has been arranged by Mr.
Candlyn. There will also be two
Indian songs, the Indian Mountain
song and the Indian Cradle song.
The chorus has improved rapidly this
year and an enjoyable program may

be expected.
