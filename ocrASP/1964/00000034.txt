New Formula For Soccer Team
Boaters lake lip Modern Dancing

A new look has been assumed by
State’s soccer team atpractlce dur-
ing the past week. It is the acquisi-
tion of Miss JoAnn Baker, Albany
State’s newest female physical ed-
ucation instructor.

Miss Baker teaches the soccer
team modern dance steps which
serve as body building exercises.
Soccer mentor Joe Garcia was quick
to point out the definite advantages
of these modern dance techniques.

"It teaches our boys to run and
jump and hop with minimum effort,"
be said. "This is a tremendous thing.
There is quite an analogy between
dance steps and the fundamental
skills needed for soccer,” Garcia
added.

Coach Garcia explained that the
simple benefits to be gained from
these steps are better coordination
and agility, both of which are neces-
sary for the rough and fast game of
soccer.

The basic steps employed by Miss
Baker are simple variations of run—
ning, hopping, and jumping mixed
with a simple shuttle. One step is
the square shuttle. It consists of
forward motion for three steps,
left for three, back for three and
right for three.

State will soon learn whether or
not the dance lessons are paying
when they travel to Oneonta tomor-
row. This will be the Feds second
game of the season.
