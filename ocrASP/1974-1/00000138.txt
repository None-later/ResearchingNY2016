Senator James Buckley‘s proposal that President Nixon resign marks the stunning

Buckley is the first Conservative to call for Mr. Nixon‘s resignation, a call which Mr,
Nixonican no longesclaim to be an attempt by liberal Democrats: to undermine the
President. The question 'of resignation is now an issue with bipartisan support,
however limited, and it is doubtless that others of both parties in the Senate also wish
the President would resign, thus saving them the trauma of having to vote on his con-
viction if impeached.

As Mr. Buckley stated, Mr. Nixon has indeed lost the capacity to govern and the
confidence of virtually the entire nation. Whether or not he has actually committed a
